# Research Plan - Technology Innovation

## Overview

This research initiative focuses on identifying disruptive technologies and concepts that create opportunities to:

1. **Disrupt established business models**
2. **Displace low value-add intermediaries**
3. **Automate manual human endeavor at scale**

## Mission

Research emerging technologies and create working prototypes backed by solid research evidence. The ultimate goal is to develop viable concepts that can be spun into new companies.

## Research Team

- **Researcher**: Technology research and analysis
- **Reports to**: CTO
- **Company**: Research Project
