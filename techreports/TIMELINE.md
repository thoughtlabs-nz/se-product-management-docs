# Timeline and Milestones

## Research Program Phases

### Phase 1: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
**Objective**: Establish research infrastructure and initial focus areas

#### Week 1: Setup & Planning
- ✅ Research team onboarded
- ✅ Documentation structure created
- ✅ Tools and access configured
- [ ] Initial technology scan completed
- [ ] Priority focus areas identified

#### Week 2: Deep Dive Planning
- [ ] Research methodology validated with CTO
- [ ] First research targets selected
- [ ] Prototype frameworks evaluated
- [ ] Communication cadence established

**Deliverables**:
- Research plan documentation (this folder)
- First technology brief
- Initial focus area prioritization

---

### Phase 2: Discovery (Weeks 3-6)
**Objective**: Survey the technology landscape and identify high-potential opportunities

#### Weekly Goals
- Deliver 2-3 technology briefs per week
- Initiate 1 deep dive research report
- Attend relevant conferences/webinars
- Build network in target domains

**Focus Areas** (select 2-3):
- AI/ML and generative AI applications
- Distributed systems and decentralization
- Developer tools and productivity
- Industry-specific automation opportunities

**Deliverables**:
- 8-12 technology briefs
- 2 deep dive research reports
- Opportunity pipeline (ranked list)

---

### Phase 3: Validation (Weeks 7-10)
**Objective**: Build prototypes and validate top opportunities

#### Prototyping Sprints
- Sprint 1 (Week 7-8): First prototype
- Sprint 2 (Week 9-10): Second prototype

**Validation Criteria**:
- Technical feasibility demonstrated
- Market opportunity sized
- Business model outlined
- Competitive landscape mapped

**Deliverables**:
- 2 working prototypes with documentation
- Technical feasibility reports
- Business case presentations
- Go/no-go recommendations

---

### Phase 4: Refinement (Weeks 11-12)
**Objective**: Refine top concepts and prepare for next steps

#### Activities
- Enhance prototype based on feedback
- Complete financial modeling
- Develop go-to-market strategy
- Prepare investor/founder materials

**Deliverables**:
- Refined prototype and documentation
- Complete business case
- Pitch deck / executive summary
- Launch plan (if pursuing)

## Success Metrics

### Quantity Metrics
- Technology briefs delivered per week: Target 2-3
- Deep dive reports per month: Target 2-3
- Prototypes built per quarter: Target 2-3

### Quality Metrics
- Percentage of briefs leading to deep dives: Target 20%

## Adjustment and Iteration

This timeline is a living document and will be updated based on:
- Findings from initial research
- Feedback from CTO and leadership
- Emerging opportunities requiring immediate attention
- Resource availability and constraints

### Review Cadence
- **Weekly**: Progress against current week's goals
- **Bi-weekly**: Phase progress and milestone tracking
- **Monthly**: Overall program health and strategic alignment

### Escalation Triggers
- Falling behind milestones by more than 1 week
- Discovery of urgent high-priority opportunity
- Resource constraints blocking progress
- Significant change in company strategy
